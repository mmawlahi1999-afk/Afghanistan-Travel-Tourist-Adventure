Your Safety Is Our Priority

Essential tips and guidelines for a secure and informed journey.

Is it safe to travel to Afghanistan right now?

We closely monitor the political and security situation. Our tours strictly follow routes approved by local authorities and we use professional, vetted security escorts. Safety protocols are updated daily.

What are the Visa and Documentation requirements?

A valid passport and a **Tourist Visa** are mandatory. We will provide all necessary invitation letters and assist you with the application process at the relevant embassy in your country.

Do I need specific Travel Insurance?

Yes. Comprehensive travel insurance covering medical emergencies, emergency repatriation, and high-risk zones is **mandatory** for all our travelers. We can recommend specialized providers.

What is the recommended dress code and cultural etiquette?

We strongly advise modesty. For women, a **headscarf** and long, loose-fitting clothing covering the arms and legs are required. For men, long trousers are expected, especially when visiting religious sites.

Where will we stay and what transport is used?

We use highly-rated, secure guesthouses and hotels in designated safe areas. All ground transportation is private, armored (if required by local protocol), and driven by experienced, professional local drivers.

What are the emergency contact procedures?

You will have a direct 24/7 line to your local guide and our operations manager. The full emergency contact list (including embassy numbers) will be provided upon arrival.

⚠️ Important Note on Flexibility

Tour itineraries may change at short notice due to local security assessments. We ask for your patience and full cooperation with your guide, whose decisions are final regarding safety protocols.
